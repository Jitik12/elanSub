Once Upon A Time...

There once lived a great Hero. He was once sharpening his sword sitting by the river when suddenly he heard the voice of a young woman calling for him. He went in search of her but couldnt find her. By the time he came back his sword and sheath were missing. He was furious and called out to the woman to return his sword. There was no reply. But the hero was not ready to give up. He went in search of her. And by the end of the day he decided to take rest next to a water fall.

As he lay down a closed his eyes he heard the mystirious voice again. "My hero, find me" it was now that he realised that the voice was coming from behind the water fall. He went carefully and found his sword stuck in the ground beside which he found a scroll.

Hello My Hero,

I am the God of Fests, or as you know me, Elan

I request for this years fest, Elan n Nvision, to happen in the most gracious of ways. I would like to help out in any way I can as well as equip you with warriors of legend that will help you in your quest.

On the day of the fest, I will be watching. If my people are not pleased then I will haunt you, mark my words. I will haunt your nights. I will devour your dreams and children. And the day when you decide to end your life, I will be there to keep you from dying, coz eternal torture is what I have for you. That is if my people are not pleased.

Yours Sincerely,
Elan ^_^
